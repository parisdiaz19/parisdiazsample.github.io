About Me

Who Am I?

I graduated from Montclair State University in 2019 with a degree in Music Education with a focus in Violin Studies. Teaching and creating has always been my passion. I decided to look into web development during my last year as an undergraduate student. Originally, I learned front-end development in order to promote a teaching business and attract students. While I was learning this skill, I realized that I wanted to share my passion and creativity for other people. I find this satisfaction in both teaching and development.

Lesson Information

I provide string lessons (violin, viola, cello) for students of all levels. I provide my services through Zoom, Skype, Discord, and in person (NJ area).
